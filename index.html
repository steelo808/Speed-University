WHAT IS SPEED UNIVERSITY
ALL ABOUT

As the founder and head coach of Rochester Speed Academy, Coach Smith has nearly 10 years of experience and knowledge coaching at the high school and club level. A highly accomplished athlete in his own right, he is committed to helping athletes reach their full potential on the track. Coach Smith primary focus is sprinters. His style includes running techniques, block techniques, and overall mechanics to ensure each athlete is successful and prepared to execute at their best.
